Programs

Awareness and Advocacy Program

  • Raises awareness about the climate crisis and the importance of environmental conservation.
  • Advocates for policies and actions that reduce greenhouse gas emissions and strengthen resilience to climate change.

Climate Resilience Program

  • Implements projects to strengthen resilience and adaptive capacity to climate-induced impacts.
  • Provides training and support for communities to prepare for and respond to climate-related disasters.
