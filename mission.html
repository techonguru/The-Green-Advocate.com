    • Mission & Vision

    Mission:

    Our mission is to raise awareness about the climate crisis and inspire people to get involved, reduce greenhouse gas emissions, and strengthen resilience and adaptive capacity to climate-induced impacts, including climate-related hazards in our sub-county. We aim to integrate climate change measures into county policies, strategies, and planning.

    Vision

    Our vision is to strengthen resilience and adaptive capacity to climate-related disasters, integrate climate measures and policies into planning, and build knowledge and capacity to meet climate change.
