About Us

The Green Advocates Organization is a non-profit organization based in Baringo County, Marigat Sub County. Our mission is to raise awareness about the climate crisis, strengthen resilience, and adaptive capacity to climate-induced impacts in our sub-county. We envision a future where resilience is strengthened, climate measures are integrated into policies and planning, and knowledge and capacity to meet climate change are built.
